Refund and Exchange Policy

Thank you for shopping at PoGo4u.

Please read this policy carefully. This is the Refund and Exchange Policy of PoGo4u.

Refund & Cancellation

Our Refund Policy lasts for 7 days after your date of purchase. If 7 days have gone by since your purchase, we cannot offer you a refund.

Our refund and cancellation Policy cannot be applied on completed and serviced orders.

To request for a refund, you must contact us at trainer@pogo4u.com with reason to your refund/cancellation proposal, along with your order ID and Pokémon Go in-game name. We also require a receipt or proof of purchase.

Once your refund/cancellation proposal is received and reviewed, we will send you an email to notify you that we have received your proposal. We will also notify you of the approval or rejection of your refund.

If you are approved, then your refund will be processed, and a credit will be refunded to your credit card or original method of payment, within a certain amount of days.

Exchange

Our Exchange Policy cannot be applied on completed and serviced orders. Please take a look at the estimated time of service provided under our products.

If you need to exchange it for a different service(s) that is equal or cheaper than the paid amount, send us an email at trainer@pogo4u.com along with your order ID, Pokémon Go in-game name, and a receipt or proof of purchase to request for an exchange.

If you have given incorrect information during your purchase or would like to transfer your purchased service(s) unto a different Pokémon Go account, please send us an e-mail as soon as possible. We will review your request and apply changes wherever possible.

Contact us

If you have any questions about our Returns and Refunds Policy, please contact us by email at trainer@pogo4u.com.
